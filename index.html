Support

Common Questions.

Everything you need to know before your journey begins.

What is included in the tour price?

Our budget tours are all-inclusive of logistics within Sri Lanka. This includes professional English-speaking local guides, 3-star boutique accommodation, all air-conditioned transportation, breakfast, and entrance fees to the major sites listed in your specific itinerary. We believe in total transparency—no hidden costs.

How do I secure my booking?

Booking is simple and secure. Once you select your package and click 'Book It Now', you'll fill in a brief interest form. A Fellow Guide will reach out within 24 hours to confirm availability and process your small deposit (usually 20%). The remaining balance is only payable upon your arrival in Sri Lanka.

Is it safe for solo budget travelers?

Absolutely. Sri Lanka is known for its legendary hospitality. Our tours are designed to be safe, social, and supportive. We vetted all our transport partners and accommodation options personally, and our 24/7 Fellow Assistant chatbot and phone support are always available during your stay.

Can I customize a pre-made package?

While our packages are curated for the best value, we love helping you make it your own. Whether you want to add an extra night in Ella or swap a temple visit for a cooking class, our team can adjust any budget plan to suit your heart's desire. Just mention it during your initial booking call.
